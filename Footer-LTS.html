  • About this website: CVS Pharmacy partners with third parties to provide this online search tool as a courtesy to help CVS Pharmacy customers identify Medicare medication coverage options. CVS Pharmacy accepts most Medicare Part D plans and does not endorse any particular plan. Your estimated costs and savings may vary depending on your premium, deductible, individual plan’s cost-sharing obligations for your prescription medications, any Medicare Part D penalty that may apply and whether you qualify for Extra Help from Medicare with paying your prescription medication costs.
  • How the online tool works: The online tool creates a list of Medicare Part D plans and Medicare Advantage plans with prescription medication coverage based on the following data: CVS Pharmacy retail patient prescription information; carrier plan information obtained from multiple sources; medicare.gov; and CMS. After entering your date of birth and ZIP code, the online tool will recommend plans based on the calculated out-of-pocket cost for medications filled at CVS Pharmacy and/or manually entered in the online tool. The tool will use information from up to 25 of your medications filled at CVS Pharmacy within the past 12 months. If the list of medications used in calculating your costs is inaccurate or incomplete, you can manually add, remove, or change information about your medications used in the online tool by clicking on “Medications”. This plan finder tool will by default display only those plans in which CVS Pharmacy is a preferred in-network pharmacy. You can select “Show All Plans” from the available plan sorting filters to view plans where CVS Pharmacy is not a preferred pharmacy.
  • How the lifestyle assessment works: You have the option to use the “Lifestyle Assessment” to answer questions about your health care coverage preferences. Your selections will be used to apply filters and sorting mechanisms to the list of recommended plans. You can edit your responses to the Lifestyle Assessment or manually apply filters and sorting preferences at any time. You can select “Show All Plans” to view all available plans in your coverage area.
  • How you can enroll in a health care plan: If you choose to enroll in a plan, you will be redirected to eHealth, a third party, to complete enrollment. CVS Pharmacy offers this online tool only as a courtesy for its customers, does not endorse any particular plan, and does not receive compensation of any kind from plan sponsors or our third party partners. Our third party partners may, however, receive compensation based on your enrollment in a particular plan.
  • About your privacy: This website uses a trusted third party source to provide information regarding some of your Medicare medication coverage options. Any information you provide through the online tool is subject to our Privacy Policy. Once you leave the online tool to go to a third party’s website, such as eHealth, the information you provide to that third party will be subject to the third party’s privacy policy, not ours. We recommend you review any third party’s privacy policy before sharing your information with them.
  • This web page is under development. If you experience difficulty using this web page or have questions regarding its content, please contact 1-888-607-4CVS (1-888-607-4287).
